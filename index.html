FAQ

How much will it cost?
$1.20/lb, 10 lb minimum (~$12). Weighed after washing and drying — you don't pay for dirt. Use the estimator.
What do I put laundry in? Do I sort it?
Anything — basket, hamper, trash bag. No sorting. Note allergies or instructions when you book. Repeat customers get a free weather-proof laundry bag.
How fast is turnaround?
Same day when possible. Afternoon pickups come back next day. Very large orders take a couple of days.
Do I need an appointment to drop off?
No — walk-ins are welcome, first come first served. You can also book a drop-off appointment, and appointments take precedence over walk-ins.
How do I pay?
Pickup orders: a secure payment link texted after weigh-in. In person: card or cash.
How far do you deliver?
Local route around Morganton and Valdese — check your zip. Drop-off has no limit.
What if something goes wrong?
We make it right. Self-service machine trouble? Call or text (828) 201-0582 with the machine number for a refund or re-run.
Stuffed animals? Pet beds?
Yes. Plushies, loveys, and dog beds of every size in our commercial machines.
